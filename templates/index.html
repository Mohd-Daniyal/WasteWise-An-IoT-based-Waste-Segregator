What we are?

Welcome to our innovative waste management solution! Our system leverages cutting-edge technologies like computer vision and IoT to revolutionize waste sorting. Using a camera feed, our system classifies waste items as biodegradable or non-biodegradable in real-time. Once classified, servo motor is activated to direct the waste items to their respective bins. This streamlined process enhances recycling efficiency and promotes environmental sustainability by ensuring that each type of waste is appropriately handled. Join us in our mission towards a greener, more sustainable world!

How it works?

To get started, follow these instructions:

  • Click the 'Next' button on the right side to proceed to the main working page of the project.
  • On the main page, click the 'Classify' button to identify the type of waste.
  • Once classified, the result will be displayed.
  • Click 'Dump' to dispose of the waste accordingly.
